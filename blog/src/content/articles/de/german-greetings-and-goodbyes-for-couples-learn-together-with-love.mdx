1. **Don't mix formal and informal**: Stick to either "Sie" (formal) or "du" (informal) throughout the conversation
2. **Time-appropriate greetings**: Don't say "Guten Morgen" in the evening
3. **Pronunciation matters**: Practice the "√º" sound in "Tsch√ºss" - it's not "oo" but a unique German sound
4. **Gender agreement**: Adjust your terms of endearment based on your partner's gender

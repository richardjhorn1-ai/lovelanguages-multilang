## How to Practice Together: Making It Natural

The key to mastering these greetings is using them daily with your partner. Start with one or two favorites and gradually expand your repertoire.

**Daily Practice Tips:**
- Choose one morning greeting to use every day this week
- Send a midday "hej" text in Swedish
- Practice pronunciation together - Swedish sounds are softer than they appear
- Create a bedtime ritual with Swedish goodnights
- Celebrate when you naturally use Swedish without thinking!

<CultureTip flag="游젏릖" title="Swedish Pronunciation Secrets">
Swedish has a musical quality - listen for the rising and falling tones. The "친" sounds like "aw" in "saw," "칛" like "eh," and "칬" like "uh" in "fur." Practice these sounds together for authentic pronunciation!
</CultureTip>

**Common Mistakes to Avoid:**
- Don't over-pronounce the "j" - it's soft like "y" in "yes"
- Swedish "r" is often rolled gently, not harshly
- Remember that Swedish uses tone to convey meaning - questions rise at the end

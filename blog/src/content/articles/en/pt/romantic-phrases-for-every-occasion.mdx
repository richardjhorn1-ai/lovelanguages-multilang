**Match the moment**: Choose phrases that fit the situation. "Estou com saudades" works perfectly when you're apart, while "VocÃª me completa" is ideal during deep conversations.

**Use authentic pronunciation**: The phonetic guides help you sound natural. Remember that Portuguese has nasal sounds - practice makes perfect!

**Be genuine**: These phrases carry emotional weight. Use them when you truly feel the sentiment to maintain their special meaning.

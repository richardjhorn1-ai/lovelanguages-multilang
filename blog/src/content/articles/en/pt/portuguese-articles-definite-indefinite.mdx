## Essential Contractions

Portuguese articles combine with prepositions to form contractions. These are mandatory, not optional.

### De (of/from) + Articles

<VocabCard
  word="Do / Da / Dos / Das"

  pronunciation="doo / dah / doosh / dahsh"
  example="O livro do professor. (The teacher's book.) De + o = do."
/>

### Em (in/on) + Articles

Portuguese verb conjugation might seem intimidating at first, but understanding the patterns makes it manageable. Portuguese verbs follow predictable rules based on their infinitive endings. Once you learn these patterns, you can conjugate thousands of verbs.

## The Three Verb Groups

Portuguese verbs are categorized by their infinitive endings. The -AR group is the largest and most common. Understanding each group's conjugation pattern is essential for speaking Portuguese fluently.

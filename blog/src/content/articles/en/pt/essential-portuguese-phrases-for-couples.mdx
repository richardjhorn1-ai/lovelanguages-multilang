## Put It Into Practice

Learning these phrases is just the beginning. Try using one new phrase each day with your partner. You might start with simple greetings and gradually work your way up to more complex expressions of love.

Remember: your partner will appreciate every effort you make, even if your pronunciation isn't perfect. The fact that you're learning their language speaks volumes about your love.

## Practice Exercises for Couples

### Exercise 1: Role-Play Restaurant Scenarios
Take turns being the waiter (**der Kellner**) and customer (**der Gast**). Practice ordering meals, asking for the menu, and paying the bill using the vocabulary you've learned.

### Exercise 2: Express Your Feelings
Each evening, tell your partner one thing you love about them using German vocabulary. Start simple with **"Ich liebe deine Augen"** (I love your eyes) and build complexity over time.

### Exercise 3: Plan a German Evening
Use German to plan your next date night together. Discuss where to go (**Wohin gehen wir?**), what to eat (**Was essen wir?**), and what activities you'd like to do (**Was machen wir?**).

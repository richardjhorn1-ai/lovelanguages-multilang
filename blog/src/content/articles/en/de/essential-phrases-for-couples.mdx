## How to Practice These Phrases Together

Learning German as a couple works best when you make it part of your daily routine. Here are some romantic ways to practice:

1. **Morning Ritual**: Start each day with "Guten Morgen, Liebling" and ask "Wie hast du geschlafen?" (How did you sleep?)
2. **Meal Practice**: Use German exclusively during one meal per day
3. **Evening Check-ins**: Share "Was war das Beste an deinem Tag?" (What was the best part of your day?)
4. **Error Celebration**: When one of you makes a mistake, respond with "Das ist okay, ich liebe dich trotzdem" (That's okay, I love you anyway)

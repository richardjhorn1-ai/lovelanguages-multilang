## How to Practice Together: Making It Natural

**Evening Tradition**: Never end a call without a proper German farewell. Make it your special thing.

**Voice Messages**: Record yourself saying these phrases. Hearing your voice adds intimacy to the learning.

**Cultural Exchange**: Ask your partner about regional greetings from their area. This shows genuine interest in their background.

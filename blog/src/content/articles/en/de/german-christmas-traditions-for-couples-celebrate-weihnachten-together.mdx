### Traditional vs. Modern Trees

German trees traditionally featured real candles (Kerzen) instead of electric lights. Some families still use real candles, keeping a bucket of water nearby! Most modern families use Lichterketten (string lights), but the traditional look remains special.

<CultureTip title="Christkind vs. Weihnachtsmann" flag="üá©üá™">
In Northern Germany, the Weihnachtsmann (Santa Claus) brings gifts. In Southern Germany and Austria, it's the Christkind (Christ Child), an angel-like figure. Both arrive on Heiligabend, not December 25th!
</CultureTip>

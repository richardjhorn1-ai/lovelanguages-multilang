## Practice Exercises for Couples

### Role-Playing Scenarios

1. **Restaurant Scene**: One partner plays the waiter, the other the customer. Practice ordering in Spanish using the vocabulary you've learned.

2. **Compliment Exchange**: Take turns giving each other compliments in Spanish using the romantic expressions.

3. **Planning Together**: Discuss your next date in Spanish, using future tense and activity vocabulary.

### Memory Games

Create flashcards with Spanish words on one side and English on the other. Quiz each other during casual moments throughout your week.

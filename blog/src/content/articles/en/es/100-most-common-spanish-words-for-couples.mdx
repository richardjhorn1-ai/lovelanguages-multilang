## Memorization Tips for Couples

1. **Flashcard dates**: Quiz each other with flashcards during dinner or walks
2. **Label your home**: Put Spanish labels on items around your house
3. **Daily goals**: Aim to use 10 words from this list each day
4. **The 5-word game**: Pick 5 random words and make a story together
5. **Spanish minutes**: Have 5 minutes of only Spanish conversation daily

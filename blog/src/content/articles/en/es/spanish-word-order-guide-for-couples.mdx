## Tips for Natural Word Order

1. **When in doubt, follow SVO** - It works most of the time
2. **Put adjectives after nouns** - Until you learn the exceptions
3. **Object pronouns before verbs** - me, te, lo, la, nos, los, las
4. **Negation before verbs** - "no" comes right before the verb
5. **Listen to native speakers** - Music and podcasts help internalize patterns

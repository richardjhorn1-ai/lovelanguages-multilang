## The Two Ways to Say I Love You

<CultureTip title="Ti amo vs Ti voglio bene" flag="üáÆüáπ">
This is the most important distinction: "Ti amo" is for deep romantic love (your partner). "Ti voglio bene" expresses caring affection (family, friends, and also partners in everyday contexts). Italians use both with their romantic partners!
</CultureTip>

## Pronunciation for English Speakers

Great news! Italian pronunciation is quite straightforward:

- **Ti** - Just like "tea"
- **amo** - "AH-mo" with a clear "ah" sound
- **voglio** - "VOH-lyoh" (the "gl" sounds like "ly")
- **bene** - "BEH-neh" with clear vowels

## Practice Exercises for Couples

### Daily Challenge Ideas:
1. **Morning Greetings**: Start each day with Italian greetings and ask "Come stai?" (How are you?)
2. **Compliment Exchange**: Give each other one Italian compliment daily
3. **Meal Planning**: Discuss dinner plans entirely in Italian
4. **Bedtime Ritual**: End each night with "Buonanotte amore mio" (Good night my love)

### Role-Playing Scenarios:
- **Restaurant Date**: Practice ordering food and complimenting each other
- **Travel Planning**: Discuss your dream Italian vacation
- **Shopping Together**: Use phrases for expressing preferences and opinions

<CultureTip title="Italian Gestures in Love" flag="ðŸ‡®ðŸ‡¹">
Italians are expressive with their hands! When saying "ti amo," try the classic Italian gesture: touch your heart with your right hand. It adds authentic Italian flair to your romantic expressions.
</CultureTip>

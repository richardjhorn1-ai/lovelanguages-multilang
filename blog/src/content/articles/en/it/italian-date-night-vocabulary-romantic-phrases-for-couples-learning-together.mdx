## Practice Exercises for Couples

### Scenario 1: Making Dinner Reservations
Practice this dialogue with your partner, taking turns being the customer and the restaurant host:

**Customer**: "Buonasera, vorrei prenotare un tavolo per due persone."
**Host**: "Certo, per che ora?"
**Customer**: "Per le otto di stasera, per favore."
**Host**: "Perfetto, a nome di chi?"

### Scenario 2: Romantic Compliments
Take turns giving each other compliments using these phrases:
- "Sei bellissima/bellissimo stasera" (You look beautiful/handsome tonight)
- "I tuoi occhi sono stupendi" (Your eyes are gorgeous)
- "Il tuo sorriso mi fa battere il cuore" (Your smile makes my heart beat faster)

### Scenario 3: Planning the Perfect Evening
Discuss your ideal date night using Italian phrases:
- "Cosa vorresti fare stasera?" (What would you like to do tonight?)
- "Che ne dici di andare a cena fuori?" (How about going out for dinner?)
- "Preferirei restare a casa e cucinare insieme" (I'd prefer to stay home and cook together)

## Why Danish Pronunciation Matters

Before diving into the sounds, understand why pronunciation is crucial in Danish. Unlike English, where mispronunciation might cause confusion, in Danish it can completely change word meanings. Getting the sounds right will help you be understood and appreciate the beautiful melody of the language.

## Common Pronunciation Mistakes

### Mistake 1: Hard D Instead of Soft D

Wrong: "God" as "god" (like English "god")
Right: "God" as "goth" (soft th sound)

### Mistake 2: Pronouncing Silent Letters

Wrong: "Jeg" as "yeg"
Right: "Jeg" as "yai"

### Mistake 3: English R Sound

Wrong: "Rigtig" with English r
Right: "Rigtig" with guttural r from throat

### The Rolling 'R'
Hungarian 'r' is lightly rolled - think of it as a gentle purr of affection. Don't overdo it! A soft tap of the tongue is perfect.

## Common Pronunciation Mistakes (And How to Avoid Them)

1. **Pronouncing 'sz' as "z"**
   - Wrong: "zeh-reh-tlek"
   - Right: "SEH-reht-lehk" (sz = s sound)

2. **Stressing the wrong syllable**
   - Wrong: "sze-RET-lek"
   - Right: "SEH-reht-lehk" (stress first syllable)

3. **Ignoring vowel length**
   - Wrong: Short √© in "szeretlek"
   - Right: Draw out that "ay" sound: "SEH"

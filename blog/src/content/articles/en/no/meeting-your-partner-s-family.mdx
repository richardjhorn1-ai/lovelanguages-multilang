### The Handshake vs. Hug Dilemma

Norwegians are generally comfortable with personal space, but family varies. Follow your partner's lead, but when in doubt, a warm handshake with eye contact and a smile works perfectly.

<CultureTip flag="游游" title="Eye Contact Matters">
In Norwegian culture, maintaining eye contact while speaking shows sincerity and interest. Don't stare intensely, but do meet their eyes when greeting and during conversation.
</CultureTip>

## Understanding Norwegian Humor

Norwegian families often bond through gentle teasing and understated humor. Don't worry if you don't catch every joke - your willingness to laugh along matters most.

### Common Playful Phrases
- "Er du sulten?" (ehr doo SOOL-tehn) - "Are you hungry?" (usually followed by offering more food!)
- "Skal vi ta en kopp?" (shahl vee tahr ehn kohp) - "Shall we have a cup?" (of coffee, always!)
- "Typisk norsk!" (TEE-pisk nohrsk) - "Typically Norwegian!" (when something goes endearingly wrong)

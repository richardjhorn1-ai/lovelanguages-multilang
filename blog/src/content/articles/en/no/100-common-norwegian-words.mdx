<CultureTip title="Norwegian Vowels" flag="游游">
Norwegian has three extra vowels: 칁 (sounds like "a" in "bad"), 칒 (like "u" in "fur"), and 칀 (like "o" in "for"). Mastering these sounds will significantly improve your pronunciation!
</CultureTip>

## Practice Strategy

Focus on learning 5-10 new words per day. Use them in sentences with your partner, and try to incorporate them into your daily conversations. Start with greetings and essential verbs, then expand to more specific vocabulary.

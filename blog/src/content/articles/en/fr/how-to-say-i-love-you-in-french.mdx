### Pronunciation Guide

The pronunciation of "Je t'aime" has sounds that don't exist in English:

- **Je** [zhuh] - Like the "s" in "measure" followed by a soft "uh"
- **t'aime** [tem] - The apostrophe shows the contraction of "te" and "aime"

**Tip**: Say "zhuh-tem" as one flowing word, almost like singing.

## The Three Levels of Love in French

<CultureTip title="Watch out for 'bien'!" flag="üá´üá∑">
In French, adding "bien" to "Je t'aime" makes it LESS intense, not more! "Je t'aime bien" means "I like you" or "I care about you" - like between friends. For romantic love, just say "Je t'aime" without "bien".
</CultureTip>

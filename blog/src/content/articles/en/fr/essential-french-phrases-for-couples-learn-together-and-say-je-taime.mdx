## Fun Practice Activities for Couples

### 1. Morning French Ritual
Start each day by greeting each other only in French until you've had breakfast together. Use phrases like **"Bonjour, mon amour"** (Good morning, my love) and **"Tu es magnifique"** (You are magnificent).

### 2. French Date Nights
Designate one evening per week as "French Night." Order French food, watch French movies with subtitles, and practice conversations about your day using newly learned phrases.

### 3. Love Note Challenge

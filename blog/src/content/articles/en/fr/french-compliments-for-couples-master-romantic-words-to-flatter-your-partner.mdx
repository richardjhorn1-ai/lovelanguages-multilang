## Practice Exercises for Couples

**Exercise 1: Daily Compliment Challenge**
Each morning, give your partner a different French compliment. Start with physical attributes, then move to personality traits, and finally to deeper expressions of love.

Leave small notes around the house with French compliments. This creates surprise moments and reinforces vocabulary through writing.

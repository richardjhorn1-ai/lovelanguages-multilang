In today's digital age, knowing how to text in French is essential for couples. French texting slang is full of creative abbreviations, phonetic spellings, and expressions that might look confusing at first but are actually quite logical once you understand the patterns. Learning to communicate digitally in French will help you stay connected with your partner throughout the day.

## Understanding French Texting Culture

French texting follows some predictable patterns that make it easier to learn:
- Numbers often replace syllables (2 = de, 1 = un)
- Letters replace sounds (C = c'est, G = j'ai)
- Words are shortened to their essential sounds
- Silent letters are dropped

The key to romantic Czech is authenticity. Start with simple expressions and build confidence together. Practice these phrases during everyday moments - while cooking, walking, or cuddling on the couch.

Czechs often express affection through actions rather than words. Bringing small gifts (kv캩tiny - flowers), remembering important dates, and showing genuine interest in your partner's day goes a long way.

**Common mistakes to avoid:**
- Don't overuse "miluji t캩" - Czechs say it less frequently than English speakers, making it more meaningful

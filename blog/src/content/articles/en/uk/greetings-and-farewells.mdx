## How to Practice Together

The best way to master these greetings is through daily practice with your partner. Start with simple "–î–æ–±—Ä–∏–π —Ä–∞–Ω–æ–∫" each morning and build from there. Create a ritual where you greet each other in Ukrainian before switching to English.

Common mistakes to avoid: Don't mix formal and informal greetings inappropriately. Use "–î–æ–±—Ä–∏–π –¥–µ–Ω—å" with your partner's parents, but save the romantic versions like "–ú–æ—î —Å–µ—Ä–¥–µ–Ω—å–∫–æ" for private moments with your loved one.

Remember, Ukrainians appreciate when foreigners make an effort with their language. Even if your pronunciation isn't perfect, your attempt will warm hearts and open doors to deeper connections.

## Showing Respect: Formal vs. Informal

Understanding when to use formal address shows cultural awareness:

**Formal (to parents, elders):**
- **–í–∏** (vih) - formal "you"
- **–ü–∞–Ω–µ [Name]** (PAH-neh) - "Mr. [Name]"
- **–ü–∞–Ω—ñ [Name]** (PAH-nee) - "Mrs. [Name]"

**Informal (to siblings, cousins):**
- **–¢–∏** (tih) - informal "you"
- **–ü—Ä–∏–≤—ñ—Ç** (prih-VEET) - casual "hi"

<CultureTip flag="üá∫üá¶" title="Respect Through Language">
Always start formal with elders. They may invite you to use informal speech‚Äîthis is an honor! Let them initiate the switch. When in doubt, stick with formal.
</CultureTip>

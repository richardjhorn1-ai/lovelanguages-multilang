<CultureTip title="Tipping in Ukraine" flag="üá∫üá¶">
Tipping (—á–∞–π–æ–≤—ñ) in Ukraine is appreciated but not as expected as in the US. 10% is generous, and rounding up the bill is common. In casual cafes, tipping isn't expected. Service charges are rarely included, unlike some European countries.

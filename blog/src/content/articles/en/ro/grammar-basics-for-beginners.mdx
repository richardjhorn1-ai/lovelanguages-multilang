## Romanian Grammar: The Simple Truth

Romanian grammar is surprisingly approachable for English speakers. Think of it like building with LEGO blocks - once you know the basic pieces, you can create anything! Unlike some languages, Romanian follows predictable patterns that make sense for couples learning together.

The secret? Romanian is a Romance language, so it shares DNA with Italian, French, and Spanish. If you've heard your partner speak any of these, you're already ahead of the game!

## The Tricky "R" Sound

<VocabCard
  word="R r"
  translation="rolled/trilled 'r' (like Spanish or Italian)"
  pronunciation="rolled r"
  example="dragoste (love) = DRAH-gohs-teh"
/>

The Romanian R is rolled/trilled, similar to Spanish or Italian. It's made by vibrating the tip of your tongue against the roof of your mouth.

**Practice tip:** Start by saying "d-d-d-d" very quickly, then try to let your tongue vibrate naturally. Practice with these words:

| Word | Meaning | Pronunciation |
|------|---------|---------------|
| dragoste | love | DRAH-gohs-teh |
| frumos | beautiful | froo-MOHS |
| verde | green | VEHR-deh |
| prieten | friend | pree-EH-ten |
| searÄƒ | evening | seh-AH-rah |

<CultureTip title="Don't Worry About the R" flag="ðŸ‡·ðŸ‡´">
If you can't roll your R's perfectly, don't stress! Romanians will still understand you, and many appreciate the effort. Your partner will find your accent charming as you learn. The key is to keep practicing!
</CultureTip>

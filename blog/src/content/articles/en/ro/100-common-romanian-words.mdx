<CultureTip title="Romanian Spelling" flag="游游">
Romanian uses special characters: 캒 (like "a" in "about"), 칙/칥 (a sound unique to Romanian), 탳 (like "sh"), and 탵 (like "ts"). Learning these sounds early will greatly improve your pronunciation.
</CultureTip>

## Study Tips

1. **Learn in context** - Practice words in sentences, not isolation
2. **Focus on high-frequency words first** - These 100 words appear constantly
3. **Practice with your partner** - Use new words in real conversations
4. **Review regularly** - Spaced repetition helps retention

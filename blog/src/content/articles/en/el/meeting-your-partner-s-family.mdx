## Before You Go: Greek Family Culture Basics

Greek families are famously warm and welcoming, but they value respect and tradition. Here's what to know before you walk through their door:

- **Bring a small gift**: Something from your country or flowers for mom
- **Dress modestly**: Greeks appreciate presentable appearance
- **Expect to eat**: Refusing food might seem rude - they love feeding guests!
- **Physical greetings**: Cheek kisses are common among family

## Pro Tips for Success

1. **Bring a small gift from your homeland** - it shows thoughtfulness
2. **Learn to say their names correctly** - practice with your partner beforehand
3. **Show interest in Greek culture** - ask about traditions, holidays, or recipes
4. **Be affectionate with your partner** - Greeks like seeing genuine love
5. **Join in family activities** - help set the table or clear dishes
6. **Laugh at yourself** - if you make language mistakes, smile and keep trying
7. **Send a thank-you message after** - a quick text in Greek shows appreciation

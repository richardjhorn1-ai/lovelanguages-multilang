## How to Use These Words on Date Night

Learning these vocabulary words is just the beginning. Here's how to make them work for your relationship:

**Start small** - Pick 5-10 words that feel natural for your typical dates. Practice saying them together while getting ready.

**Create context** - Use the restaurant vocabulary when you're actually dining out, or the entertainment words when planning your next movie night.

**Make it playful** - Turn learning into a game. Quiz each other, or award points for using new words naturally during your date.

**Be patient** - Greek pronunciation takes practice. Your partner will appreciate your effort more than perfection.

**Connect emotionally** - These aren't just vocabulary words - they're tools to deepen your connection. Use them to express care and create shared experiences.

For more romantic phrases to complement your vocabulary, check out our guide to [Greek romantic phrases for every occasion](/learn/en/el/greek-romantic-phrases-every-occasion/).

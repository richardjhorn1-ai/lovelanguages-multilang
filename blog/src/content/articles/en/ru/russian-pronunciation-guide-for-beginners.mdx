## Pronunciation Rules Summary

### Always True

1. **Stressed –û** = "oh"
2. **Unstressed –û** = "ah"
3. **–ï** = "ye" (or "e" after —á, —à, –∂, —â)
4. **Final voiced consonants** become voiceless
5. **–¨** (soft sign) softens the preceding consonant

## Key Takeaways

1. **Learn the alphabet first** - it's your foundation
2. **Watch false friends** - –í, –ù, –†, –° look familiar but sound different
3. **Master vowel reduction** - unstressed –û becomes –ê
4. **Don't fear –´** - practice and patience work
5. **Listen to natives** - your partner is your best teacher!

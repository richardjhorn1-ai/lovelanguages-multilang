## Before You Arrive: Turkish Family Culture Essentials

Turkish families are known for their incredible hospitality and strong family bonds. Understanding a few cultural basics will help you make the best first impression:

- **Remove your shoes** when entering the home
- **Bring a small gift** - flowers for mom, Turkish delight for the family
- **Elders first** - greet the oldest family members before others
- **Accept offered food and drinks** - it's a sign of respect and hospitality

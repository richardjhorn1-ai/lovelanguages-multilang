<CultureTip title="Who Pays?" flag="ðŸ‡¹ðŸ‡·">
In Turkish culture, there is often a friendly "fight" over who pays the bill. It is considered polite to insist on paying. If your partner insists, you might say "Tamam, ama bir dahaki sefere ben" (Okay, but next time it's on me). This dance of generosity is part of Turkish hospitality culture.
</CultureTip>

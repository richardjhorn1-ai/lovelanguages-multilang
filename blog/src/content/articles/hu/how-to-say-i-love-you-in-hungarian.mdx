## The Verb "Szeretni" - To Love

Understanding **szeretni** (to love) helps you express your feelings in many contexts. Hungarian verb conjugation is unique because the ending changes based on who you are talking to:

| Person | Hungarian | English |
|--------|-----------|---------|
| I love you (informal) | szeretlek | I love you |
| I love him/her | szeretem | I love him/her |
| You love me | szeretsz | you love me |
| He/She loves | szeret | he/she loves |
| We love | szeretj√ºk | we love |
| They love | szeretik | they love |

<CultureTip title="Definite vs Indefinite" flag="üá≠üá∫">
Hungarian has two types of verb conjugation: definite and indefinite. When you say **szeretlek** (I love you), the **-lek** ending indicates both the subject (I) and the object (you) in a single word. This is beautifully efficient and deeply personal.
</CultureTip>

## Practice Makes Perfect

The best way to learn these phrases is to use them genuinely. Start with **szeretlek** and say it when you truly feel it. Your Hungarian partner will appreciate the effort, even if your pronunciation needs work.

Remember these tips for better pronunciation:

- The **sz** in Hungarian sounds like the English "s" in "sun"
- The **s** alone sounds like "sh" in "shop"
- Roll the **r** slightly
- Stress is always on the first syllable

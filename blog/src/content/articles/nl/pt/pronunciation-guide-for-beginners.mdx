## The Sweet Sound of Portuguese: Why Pronunciation Matters for Couples

## The Portuguese Alphabet: Your Love Letters

### Vowels: The Heart of Portuguese
Portuguese vowels are pure and open, creating that passionate sound:

- **A** - like "a" in "car" (but more open): **casa** (KA-sah) - house

## Stress and Rhythm: The Music of Love

Portuguese words have specific stress patterns that create their musical rhythm:

1. **Words ending in vowels, -m, or -s**: stress the second-to-last syllable

2. **Words ending in other consonants**: stress the last syllable

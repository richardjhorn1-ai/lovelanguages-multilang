## How to Practice These Together

Make these greetings part of your daily routine! Start with simple **"Bonjour"** and **"Bonsoir"** each day, then gradually add the romantic variations. Practice pronouncing them together - French pronunciation can be tricky for Romanian speakers, especially the nasal sounds.

**Common mistakes to avoid:**
- Don't pronounce the final consonants in French (unlike Romanian)
- The French "r" is throaty, not rolled like in Romanian
- "Bonjour" is never used after 6 PM - switch to "Bonsoir"

## Structure

### 1. Opening Hook (1 paragraph)
- Relatable scenario couples face
- Why this guide will help
- Promise of confidence/competence
- Link to related article

### 2. PhraseOfDay Component
- Most useful phrase for this situation
- One they'll use immediately

### 3. Before You Go / Preparation
- What to know beforehand
- Cultural expectations
- VocabCard for key phrase
- CultureTip about etiquette

### 4. Essential Phrases (Table)
- 15-20 must-know phrases
- Phrase | Pronunciation | Translation | Situation
- Grouped by sub-situation

### 5. Common Scenarios
- 3-4 specific scenarios within the topic
- Each with:
  - Mini dialogue example
  - Key phrases
  - What to expect

### 6. Vocabulary List
- 20-30 relevant nouns/verbs
- Table format
- Grouped by category

### 7. Polite Phrases & Formality
- How to be polite in this context
- Formal vs informal versions
- CultureTip about expectations
- VocabCard for polite phrase

### 8. What If Things Go Wrong?
- Phrases for problems/confusion
- "I don't understand"
- "Can you help me?"
- Emergency phrases if relevant

### 9. Pro Tips
- 5-7 insider tips
- Things only locals know
- Partner can help with these
- CultureTip

### 10. Quick Reference Card
- Summary of top 10 phrases
- Easy to screenshot
- Closing + CTA

# SEO Article Improvement Guide

> Master template for improving Love Languages blog articles for better CTR and conversions.

## About Love Languages (Product Context)

Love Languages is a language learning app for **mixed-language couples** - where one partner speaks the target language natively.

**306 language pairs** (18 languages × 17 targets): EN, ES, FR, DE, IT, PT, RO, NL, SV, NO, DA, PL, CS, RU, UK, EL, HU, TR

### Key Features (Use in CTAs)

| Feature | What It Does | Best For |
|---------|--------------|----------|
| **AI Voice Chat ("Cupid")** | Practice speaking, AI responds in native language | Pronunciation practice, speaking confidence |
| **Listen Mode** | Capture real conversations with live transcription/translation | Family dinners, video calls, TV shows |
| **Tutor Role** | Native partner creates challenges, tracks progress | Partner involvement |
| **Word Gifts** | Partner sends vocabulary as gifts | Pet names, romantic terms |
| **Love Log** | Personal vocabulary tracker with spaced repetition | All articles |
| **5 Game Modes** | Flashcards, multiple choice, type-it, quick-fire, verb mastery | Practice/learning |

### Pricing
- Standard: $9.99/mo (2000 words, 60 min voice)
- Unlimited: $14.99/mo (unlimited everything)
- **Partner included FREE**

### Unique vs Competitors
- Built for couples, not individuals
- Partner actively participates as tutor
- Listen Mode for real-world immersion (unique!)
- Romantic/relationship content

## Title Improvements

### Rules
- **Under 60 characters** (Google truncates longer)
- **Add number** when applicable (50+, 75, 100)
- **Add emotional hook** (Enamorar, Melt Heart, Charmer)
- **Focus on outcome**, not just content

## Meta Description Improvements

### Rules
- **Under 155 characters**
- **Start with action verb** (Learn, Master, Surprise, Impress, Make)
- **Mention relationship context** (partner, family, in-laws)
- **Include specific phrase** from target language
- **Add emotional benefit**

### By Article Type

| Type | Pattern |

## Content Improvements

### Add These Sections (if missing)

1. **"When to Use" Context**
   - In private vs with family vs in public vs texting
   - Formal vs informal situations

2. **Dialogue Examples**
   - Morning routine conversation
   - Evening check-in
   - Romantic moment

3. **Cultural Media References**
   - TV shows/series in that language
   - Popular songs
   - Movies

4. **Bridge Section Before CTA**
   - "From Reading to Speaking" or similar
   - Acknowledge the challenge (pronunciation, script, etc.)
   - Connect to the app as solution

## CTA Improvements

### Rules
- **NEVER use generic `<CTA />`**
- **Mention 2-3 specific features** relevant to article topic
- **Match feature to user problem**
- **Localize button text** to article's native language

### Feature Matching by Article Type

| Article Type | Best Features to Mention |
|--------------|-------------------------|
| Pet names | Word Gifts from partner, AI voice for pronunciation |
| I love you | AI voice chat (say it perfectly), Tutor role |
| Essential phrases | Listen Mode (family dinners), Love Log |
| Meeting family | Listen Mode (real conversations), Tutor creates challenges |
| Date night | AI voice practice, Love Log vocabulary |
| Compliments | Word Gifts, AI voice for delivery |
| Romantic phrases | AI voice chat, Partner as tutor |

## Quality Checklist

Before saving an improved article:

- [ ] Title under 60 characters with emotional hook
- [ ] Meta description under 155 characters with action verb
- [ ] Content has "when to use" context
- [ ] At least one dialogue example exists
- [ ] CTA mentions 2-3 specific features
- [ ] CTA button text is in native language
- [ ] All content is in the article's native language
- [ ] Cultural references are accurate for target language

# Spanish Date Night Vocabulary

Spanish-speaking cultures know how to romance! Whether you're enjoying tapas in Spain or a cozy dinner in Latin America, these phrases will make your date night unforgettable.

<CultureTip>
In Spain and Latin America, dinner is often late (9-10 PM in Spain!) and leisurely. The waiter won't rush you or bring the check until you ask. It's considered rude to hurry diners!
</CultureTip>

### Drink Orders

| Spanish | English |
|---------|---------|
| Una copa de vino | A glass of wine |
| Una botella de tinto | A bottle of red wine |
| Una botella de blanco | A bottle of white wine |
| Dos cervezas, por favor | Two beers, please |
| Un cóctel | A cocktail |
| Agua, por favor | Water, please |

<CultureTip>
In Spain, making eye contact while toasting is important! The traditional toast is "¡Salud!" (health), but in romantic settings, "¡Por nosotros!" (to us) or "¡Por el amor!" (to love) is perfect.
</CultureTip>

Learning French for your partner? French is called the language of love for a reason. These essential words form the foundation of everyday conversation and will help you connect with your French-speaking loved one.

<CultureTip>
In French, "tu" (informal you) is used with people you're close to—family, friends, and romantic partners. Using "tu" with your partner shows intimacy. "Vous" is for formal situations or strangers.
</CultureTip>

## Practice Tips

The best way to learn these words is through daily use:

1. **Morning greetings** - Say "Bonjour mon amour, je t'aime" every morning
2. **Evening conversations** - Share your day in French
3. **Text messages** - Send sweet French messages
4. **French films** - Watch movies with subtitles

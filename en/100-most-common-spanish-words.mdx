<CultureTip>
Spanish has two verbs for "to be": "ser" (permanent traits) and "estar" (temporary states). You'd say "Eres hermosa" (you ARE beautiful - it's who you are) vs "Estás hermosa" (you LOOK beautiful - tonight).
</CultureTip>

## Practice Tips

The best way to learn these words is through daily practice:

1. **Morning greetings** - Say "Buenos días mi amor, te quiero" every morning
2. **Evening conversations** - Share your day in Spanish
3. **Text messages** - Send sweet Spanish messages
4. **Spanish media** - Watch telenovelas with subtitles

# 100 Most Common German Words

Learning German for your partner? These foundational words form the backbone of everyday German conversation. Master these, and you'll understand a surprising amount of what your German-speaking loved one says.

<CultureTip>
Germans distinguish between "Ich liebe dich" (romantic love) and "Ich hab dich lieb" (affectionate love). The latter is less intense but still meaningful—perfect for family or early relationships.
</CultureTip>

## Practice Tips

The best way to learn these words is to use them daily with your partner:

1. **Morning greetings** - Say "Guten Morgen, ich liebe dich" every morning
2. **Evening conversations** - Share your day in German
3. **Text messages** - Send short German messages throughout the day
4. **Movies together** - Watch German films with subtitles

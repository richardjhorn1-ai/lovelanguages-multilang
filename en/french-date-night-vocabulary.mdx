# French Date Night Vocabulary

Romance and France go hand in hand. Whether you're enjoying a candlelit dinner or a moonlit walk along the Seine, these French phrases will make your date night truly magical.

<CultureTip>
In France, the server won't bring the check until you ask. This isn't neglect—it's politeness! The French don't rush diners. When ready, catch their eye and say "L'addition, s'il vous plaît."
</CultureTip>

### Drink Orders

| French | English |
|--------|---------|
| Un verre de vin | A glass of wine |
| Une bouteille de rouge | A bottle of red |
| Une bouteille de blanc | A bottle of white |
| Du champagne | Champagne |
| Un cocktail | A cocktail |
| De l'eau, s'il vous plaît | Water, please |

<CultureTip>
When clinking glasses in France, always make eye contact! It's considered bad luck not to, and some say it results in seven years of bad sex. Also, never cross arms with others while toasting.
</CultureTip>
